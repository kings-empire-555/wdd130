Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa. It is bordered by Niger to the north, Chad to the northeast, Cameroon to the east, and Benin to the west. To the south, Nigeria is surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean along its Gulf of Guinea coastline. As Africa's most populous country, Nigeria is a hub of economic activity, with key sectors including oil production, agriculture, telecommunications, and entertainment. The country is also rich in cultural diversity, with over 250 ethnic groups. Abuja is the capital city, while Lagos is Nigeria's largest city and a major financial center.
